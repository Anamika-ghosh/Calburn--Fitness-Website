HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)

HIIT involves short bursts of intense exercise followed by periods of rest. It's effective for burning calories and improving cardiovascular fitness.

Pilates

Pilates focuses on core strength, flexibility, and overall body awareness. It includes exercises like the plank, leg circles, and the hundred.

Circuit Training

Circuit training combines strength and aerobic exercises in a sequence. It's a versatile workout that targets different muscle groups.

Swimming

Swimming is a full-body workout that improves cardiovascular fitness and builds muscle strength. It's a low-impact exercise suitable for all ages.

Zumba

Zumba is a dance-based workout that combines Latin and international music with dance moves. It's a fun way to improve cardiovascular fitness.

Bodyweight Exercises

Bodyweight exercises use your own body for resistance. Examples include squats, lunges, and push-ups. They are effective for strength training.

Cardio Workout

Cardio exercises help improve cardiovascular health and burn calories. Examples include running, cycling, and jumping jacks.

Yoga

Yoga improves flexibility, balance, and mental well-being. Poses like downward dog and warrior pose are popular in yoga workouts.

Strength Training

Strength training builds muscle and increases metabolism. Common exercises include weight lifting, push-ups, and squats.
